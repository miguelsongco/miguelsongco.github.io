Capstone: VR Arcade Classics (Pac-Man & Frogger)

The VR Arcade Classics project is a full-scale recreation of two legendary arcade games—Pac-Man and Frogger—designed for immersive Virtual Reality using Unity and Quest 2 headsets. Our goal is to blend nostalgic gameplay with cutting-edge VR technology, delivering a smooth, engaging player experience.

Project Highlights

  • Pac-Man VR: Ghost AI (Blinky, Pinky, Inky, Clyde), scared mode mechanics, VR player controls, immersive UI.
  • Frogger VR: Procedural generation for road/river obstacles, 3D environments, collision mechanics for VR movement.
  • Optimization for smooth gameplay on Quest 2 (targeting 60 FPS).

My Contributions

  • Engineered character animations and enemy mechanics (Pac-Man).
  • Led level design & obstacle logic (Frogger).
  • Handled performance profiling and VR hardware optimization.
