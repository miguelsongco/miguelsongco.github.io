Welcome to Your Fitness Journey!

Overview:

This application will allow you to create a profile with us that we will save and adjust a workout plan according to your preferences and how many days you want to workout per week. You will be able to adjust any information you give us within the dashboard and workout sections after you created a profile in the profile creation tab. Your dashboard will show your information, goals, and workout plan. To generate a workout plan, Please create a profile with workout preferences and how many days per week.

Privacy Policy: We collect your personal information solely to customize your workout experience. Once you edit your preferences, previous data is promptly deleted. We do not share your information with third parties. Your privacy matters to us.

Profile Creation

Create a profile using their this link or the 'Profile Creation' tab. Once you are on the page, enter your information. Please refer to the privacy policy for any information regarding your personal information.
